FAQ
How is the score calculated?

The score ranks on a total of 10 points the quality of the free/open source licensing. It will attribute points to the number of files with a declared license on their headers, count as points the copyright assignments and check if exists documentation to help identify the usage and distribution conditions for a given piece of software. The last step is scoring the originality of the code available and how well identified are the third-party components.
